Welcome to SJDC

St.Joseph's Degree College, a prestigious higher education institution, was founded in Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, in 1994. The institute offers 12 UG and 3 PG Degree programmes for students to further their studies. These programs are specially designed for Full Time mode. The institute offers a variety of courses such as M.Sc., M.Com, B.Sc., B.Com, BBA in major streams including Science, Accounting & Commerce, Business & Management Studies. St.Joseph's Degree College provides its students with top-notch education delivered by its skilled and experienced faculty in the fields of Mathematics, Microbiology
